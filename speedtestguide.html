Chapter 2 Internet Speed

Internet speed comes in many types based on the type of internet: Cell Internet Satellite Internet DSL Internet Fiber optic internet Dial Up Other Means Cell internet or Phone Data as some call it is transmitted using cell towers from the area you are located.
Generation 2G 3G 4G 4G LTE
Speed 9.6-14.4 kbps 500-700 kbps 1-3Mbps 3-5 Mbps
The other various types are "Home" Based types meaning you can't take them with you. The Mother of today's internet comes from Dial-Up. What is Dial-Up? Dial-Up is internet provided through your home phone line. It is very poor quality, but is still commonly used today. The "General" type of internet is DSL or Fiber Optic. DSL or Digital Subscriber Line is one of the most powerful standards in internet. This is limited to mostly city limits, and isn't widely available to rural areas. Fiber Optic or FIOS meaning Fiber Optic Services, is the same as DSL but, can reach peaker speeds. Satellite internet, which is the revolution in technology is actually meant to become a replacement of DSL or FIOS and is available anywhere (Exclusions apply) is the moderate quality internet. This is because you are limited on what you can do, you have a data cap like on your cell phone, making it hard to get important computer updates or anything really at all. Other Means of internet are things like Wireless Broadband which is usually dispensed by a Antenna and communicates to various towers.
